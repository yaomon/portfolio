Kyle Sun is a Taiwan born, U.S. based Embedded Software Engineer and Freelance Artist. Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and self taught artist of over a decade. His passions are in any creative endeavor, be it art, programming, storytelling, or even cooking. He specializes in Web Development/Design and Game Development. His well-rounded experience from front-end to back-end gives him a unique perspective to be able to solve problems and facilitate communication between teams.

Kyle is currently employed full-time as an embedded software engineer working on a variety of embedded devices in fields ranging from civil to medical. His work has provided him with opprotunities to touch all branches of software and even some hardware. He has made front-end web UIs, back-end cloud services, unit-test suites, and numerous embedded functions on bare-metal devices including FOTA and custom lightweight GUI libraries .
